Administrative Functions

This functionality is available to the Financial Sponsor Coverage Group only.

Financial Sponsor administrators have additional rights to modify CapIQ company information from within the CapIQ Company Page (see Access the CapIQ Company Page)

Add New Investment

  1. Access the CapIQ Company Page
  2. Scroll down to the "CapIQ Information" section
  3. <New Investment>
  4. Click to search and add Sponsor or Portfolio companies
  5. Fill in all other relevant fields (I = Required Fields)
  6. <Save> (or <Save & New>) to add in another investment

Edit (or Delete) Investment

  1. Access the CapIQ Company Page
  2. Scroll down to the "CapIQ Information" section
  3. Click the "Edit" (or "Del") next to the investment to update
  4. Update investment information
  5. <Save>

Add Funds

  1. Access the CapIQ Company Page
  2. Hover over "Funds" Quick Link(at top)
  3. <New Fund>
  4. Enter fund information
  5. <Save> (<Save & New> to add another fund)

Edit (or Delete) Funds

  1. Access the CapIQ Company Page
  2. Hover over "Funds" Quick Link(at top)
  3. Click "Edit" (or "del") next to fund to edit
  4. Update fund information
  5. <Save>
