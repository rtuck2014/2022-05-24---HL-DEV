Updating Salesforce1 for Latest iOS Release

Ensure the latest iOS update is installed on the iOS Mobile device (Go to Settings | General | click on "Software Update")

 

Step

Before you begin:

  1. Clear the cache data on the Mobile device and re-install Salesforce1 app:
  2. Close the "Salesforce1" app
  3. Delete the "Salesforce1" app, by holding down on the Salesforce1 app, and selecting the "x" on top of the app icon
  4. Click on the "Settings" app
  5. Scroll down and click on "Safari"
  6. Scroll down and click on "Clear History", click on "Clear History" again to confirm
  7. Click on "Clear Cookies and Data", click on "Clear History" again to confirm NOTE: It might also say "Clear History and Website Data" (depending on the iOS version). If so, click on that to clear the history and website data.
  8. Download the latest Salesforce1 app from the App Store again
Step Action

Launch and Log into Salesforce1 app:

1. Click the Menu symbol (these screenshots are from a phone and may appear slightly different on your device.)

2. Select the plus sign to add a connection

3. Enter the connection information

  • Host: hl.my.salesforce.com
  • Label: HL Salesforce

4. Click “Done”

5. Enter your User Name and Password on the single sign on screen

6. Click “Sign In”

7. Click “Allow”
