The flagging feature in Salesforce is a way to alert the data hygiene team that there is an issue with a particular record.

Contact Flags

Here are some examples of some contact flags:

Scenario Flag Reason Flag Reason Comment
Contact has retired Retired Used to work at Waterman Pipes. www.watermanpipes/aboutus/founder-retires/
Contact has past away Deceased Used to work at NYC Department of Energy. www.nytimes/obituaries/steve-clark/
After extensive searching you are unable to confirm a current email address Researched - With No Results Unable to find any additional information.
Able to positively confirm an email address Researched - Email Correct Confirmed email address on company website.
