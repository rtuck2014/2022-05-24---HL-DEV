Conflicts Checks Guidelines (11/19)

It is imperative to follow these guidelines when submitting Conflicts Checks!

Company Name

  • Provide the full, legal name and include any known d/b/a's and/or f/k/a's
  • Include any name that may be used as a synonym for the name of the interested party

Example 1: Use "Fortress Investments" instead of just "Fortress" Example 2: Include "Aluminum Company of America" as well as "ALCOA".

Individual Name Guidelines

  • Identify an individual by his/her full name, including middle initial or middle name
  • When entering an individual first and last name, the company with which the individual is associated (e.g. employer) should be entered as well
  • If an associated company name is not available, then the "no associated company" box should be checked allowing the Company Name field to be left blank

Position Guidelines

  • Client - Parties associated with the client, such as (to the extent known) a subsidiary, and whose interests are aligned with the client (for example, being on the same side of a transaction as the client)
  • Adverse - Parties whose interests are adverse to the client (for example, being on the other side of a transaction or litigation with the client)
  • Other - Any other potentially interested party that should be run through the conflicts check system, such as (to the extent known) a controlling or significant shareholder or sponsor

Explanation Guidelines

This section is used to describe the named party's relationship with the client, subject, adverse party or other interested party or to otherwise explain the named party's involvement or role in the engagement or investment.
