Sync Pre-Existing Activity with Meeting Notes

You can sync a pre-existing activity in Salesforce to Outlook and add meeting notes:

  1. Lookup the contact
  2. Hover over the Activities "Quick-Link"
  3. Click "Edit" for the activity you wish to sync
  4. Click the "Sync to Outlook" checkbox
  5. Enter any meeting details in the "Internal Notes" field (not "Description" field as these may sync to external contacts)
  6. If you would like to also schedule and sync a follow-up, scroll down to the Schedule Followup section and add in a followup event and make sure to check the "Sync to Outlook" box (please note, the system does not automatically send out the invitations...you have to do it manually from Outlook)
  7. <Save>
  8. Activity is now synced with Outlook of the Primary HL Attendee

Salesforce will not automatically send the scheduled followup. You must open the follow-up calendar event created in Outlook, add the recipients manually and "send" the invite from within Outlook.

For further Outlook syncing information, please see Salesforce Sync to Outlook.

Additional Activity Syncing Information

  • Synced Activities will only sync to the Outlook calendar of the primary HL Attendee.
  • External and HL attendees will be included in the Outlook calendar “To” line but the invitation will not be automatically sent. The calendar item must be opened and then invitations can be sent, you will be prompted to send when closing the calendar item
  • To sync an Outlook Calendar item to Salesforce, set the category to Salesforce using the Categorize icon in Outlook shown below. All HL invitees to the meeting will see the activity in their activities in Salesforce. The HL primary attendee can edit the activity in Salesforce
  • Changes made to a synced activity in Salesforce will update the calendar of the primary HL attendee only. Changes made to the Outlook calendar of the primary HL attendee only will sync to Salesforce
  • In Salesforce, check the Sync to Outlook check box on the activity and it will sync to the Outlook calendar of the primary HL Attendee only. Both external and HL attendees will be included in the Outlook calendar “To” line but the invitation will not be automatically sent. The calendar item must be opened and then invitations can be sent, you will be prompted to send when closing the calendar item
  • If you can’t find a Salesforce activity on your calendar, check the primary attendee of the activity in Salesforce. It may be the wrong person or the invitation may not have been sent from Outlook
  • To sync an Outlook Calendar item to Salesforce, set the category to Salesforce using the Categorize icon in Outlook shown below. All HL invitees to the meeting will see the activity in their activities in Salesforce. The HL primary attendee can edit the activity in Salesforce
  • Changes made to a synced activity in Salesforce will update the calendar of the primary HL attendee only. Changes made to the Outlook calendar of the primary HL attendee only will sync to Salesforce

Note: Whoever is sending the meeting invitations should be the HL primary attendee – in case a delegate is sending the invitations, their officer should be the HL attendee
