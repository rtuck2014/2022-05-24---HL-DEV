Create a New Company Record

Watch the How-To Video

Important: Do Not Create Duplicate Records! Before you create a new record, be sure to search Salesforce and verify the record does not already exist (see Searching).

Create a New Company

  1. Click "Companies"tab
  2. <Add Company>
  3. Select the "Record Type" then <Continue>:
    • Operating Company (default) - Companies that make goods or provide services (ex. Information Technology, Consumer Products, Healthcare, Law firm, Accounting, etc.) These are often Portfolio Companies of Private Equity firms or Hedge Funds, prospective, engaged and/or former clients.
    • Capital Provider - Private Equity Firms, Hedge Funds, Venture Capital firms and Banks.
  4. Enter all company information in the supplied fields (I = Required Fields)
  5. <Save>
  6. Duplicate Alert - If the system identifies you are trying to add a duplicate record follow these additional steps:
    1. Review the companies identified as potential duplicates (tip: click on the company link to view in a new tab).
    2. If the company already exists, click <Cancel> and update the existing record (if necessary).
    3. If the company doesn't exist, click <Save (Ignore Alert)> to create a new record.

FS Coverage - When entering new companies, use the following record types:Portfolio Companies = Operating Company and Sponsor Companies = Capital Provider

Add Contacts to the New Company

  1. Once the company is created, mouseover the " Contact" quick-link
  2. Click <New Contact>
  3. Enter all contact information in the supplied fields (be sure to add HL Relationships)

Add New CapIQ Company to Salesforce

CapIQ records are not actual Salesforce records and only exist within the CapIQ Company Information page.

If the company you wish to enter in Salesforce already exists but is listed as a CapIQ company, create the new record using the "Add Salesforce Company" function on the CapIQ page. This will create a link between the new Salesforce record and the CapIQ company record. Here's how:

  1. Lookup the Sponsor company in Salesforce using the global search.
  2. Click the company link in the "CapIQ Companies" area.
  3. Click <Add Salesforce Company> (see screenshot below)
  4. Enter all required company information and any additional company information.
  5. <Save>
