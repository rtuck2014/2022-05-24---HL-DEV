Campaign Reports

This section will show you how to run a campaign reports for the purposes of viewing and printing out a campaign list.

Standard Report (Easiest)

  1. Lookup the campaign name in the Global Search
  2. Open the campaign
  3. Scroll down to the System Information section and click the triangle on the left to expand the section
  4. Under Custom Links, click “View All Campaign Members”

Custom Report

  1. Click tab
  2. Select from the folder list in the left-hand sidebar
  3. Find Template Name = "TEMPLATE - 2015 [xx] Conference_ALL" (type "template" in the search box)
  4. Click and select "Customize"
  5. In the "Select Campaign" field, click
  6. Select "All Campaigns" from the campaign list, type in the campaign name then <Go!>
  7. Click on the Campaign Link
  9. Select <Printable View> or <Export Details> to export to Excel
