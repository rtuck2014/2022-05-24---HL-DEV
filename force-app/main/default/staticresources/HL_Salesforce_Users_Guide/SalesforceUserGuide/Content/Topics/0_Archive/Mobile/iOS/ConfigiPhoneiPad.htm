Configuring Salesforce for the iPhone and iPad

Ensure the Salesforce1 app is installed on the device. If not, please go to the app store and download it. (See Updating Salesforce1 for Latest iOS Release for directions on downloading the Salesforce1 app)

Step Action

Launch and Log into Salesforce1 app:

1. Click the Menu symbol (these screenshots are from a phone and may appear slightly different on your device.)

2. Select the plus sign to add a connection

3. Enter the connection information

  • Host: hl.my.salesforce.com
  • Label: HL Salesforce

4. Click “Done”

5. Enter your User Name and Password on the single sign on screen

6. Click “Sign In”

7. Click “Allow”
