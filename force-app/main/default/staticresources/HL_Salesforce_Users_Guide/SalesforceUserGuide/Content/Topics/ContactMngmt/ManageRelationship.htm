Manage HL Relationships

Note: It is highly recommended to add a "Strength Rating" to the relationship. This helps others identify the relationship level as well as providing a better way to target contacts for reporting or marketing purposes.

  1. From the contact page, hover over the "HL Relationships" quick-link.

  2. <New Relationship> (or select "Edit" or "Delete" to modify existing relationships)
  3. Enter the last name of HL staff member then click
  4. Select the appropriate staff member from "Search Results" area
  5. Type - enter the type of contact (required)
  6. Strength Rating:
    1. High - contact would return your call
    2. Medium - have met the contact and they would know who you are
    3. Low - you have an association with the contact
  7. Sync to Outlook - this box is checked by default and should remain checked if you currently sync your Salesforce contacts to Outlook (and thus sync your mobile device to your Outlook contacts). Those accessing their contacts exclusively via HL Force and the mobile app (Salesforce 1) will not need to check this box. For more information, please see refer to Salesforce Sync to Outlook.
  8. Personal Notes - enter notes visible by only you or your delegate (syncs to your "Notes" field in Outlook)
  9. Outlook Categories - if you sync to Outlook, you can apply a category to the record in Outlook. Categories must be entered exactly as they appear in Outlook and multiple categories must be separated by commas (e.g. Holiday,Healthcare,New Clients)
  10. <Save>
