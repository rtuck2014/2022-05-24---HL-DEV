Engagement Summary (CF & FR)

The Engagement Summary functionality located in the engagement area of Salesforce allows CF & FR staff members to record and track specific required engagement information. To update this information, follow these steps:

  1. Lookup the engagement
  2. <CF Engagement Summary> or <FR Engagement Summary> (top of page)
  3. Enter in all the engagement summary information utilizing the multiple tabs at the top
  4. When finished, click <Save>
  5. <Return to Engagement>

CF Specifics:

  • Required Field - Be sure to fill out the "Companies Closed With" field located in Engagements > Counterparty Statistics.
  • The engagement summary must be completed within 30 days of the deal closing date.
