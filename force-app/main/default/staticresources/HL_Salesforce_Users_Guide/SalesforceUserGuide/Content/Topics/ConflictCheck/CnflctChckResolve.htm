Resolving Conflicts Checks (11/19)

How to Resolve a Conflicts Check

  1. Deal team receives an email when relationships with one or more interested parties have been found
  2. Click the "Click here to update the final status" hyperlink located in the email
  3. Go to the Final Disposition tab
  4. Click <View Conflicts Check Report> to review the report, pay specific attention to the "Search Results" sections and be sure to review ALL pages of the report
  5. Return to the Final Disposition tab
  6. Enter the appropriate "Resolution Status" based on the report:
    • Cleared - no conflict exists
    • Managed - additional steps need to be taken to manage a potential conflict (e.g. cleared with waivers, disclosure, clarification, etc.)
    • True Conflict - signifies we should not move forward with the opportunity or engagement
  7. Select the "Authorizing Officer"
  8. Enter any relevant notes in the "Conflicts Resolution Comment" field
  9. <Save Comments and Update Status>

Additional Options on Final Disposition Tab

  • Email Deal Team Members - opens an email addressed to the deal team members
  • Save Comments and Mark Pending - saves your updates but does not submit the resolution status
  • Return to Conflicts Admin - sends the conflicts check back to the Conflicts Check Administrator. Be sure to include the reasons for the return in the "comments" area
