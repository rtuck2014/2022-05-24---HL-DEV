Campaign Hierarchy and Structure

Campaigns have been designed via a format that will ensure tracking of historical information as well as provide more depth into any given campaign.

Parent campaigns are used as a shell to house all sub-parent and child campaigns.

Conferences & Events - the sub-parent campaign will always refresh with the most recent contact status.

Every campaign requires a parent campaign!
