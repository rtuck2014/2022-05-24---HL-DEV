Billing Requests (FVA, CF and SC only)

  1. Lookup the engagement.
  2. <Billing Request> (at top)
  3. To - contact to receive the bill, clickand search for contact in Salesforce (or type email address directly in the field)
  4. Additional CCs - click to expand then enter additional contacts to CC on the email
  5. Attachment - <Choose File> to attach a file
  6. Subject - this will be the email subject line
  7. Email Body - update billing language and fees directly in the bill template (below formatting controls)
  8. <Send Email>
