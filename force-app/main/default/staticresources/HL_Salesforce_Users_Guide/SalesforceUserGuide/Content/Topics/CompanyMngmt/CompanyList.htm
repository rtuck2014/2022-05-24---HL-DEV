Company Lists

The Company List feature allows staff members to create customized groups of companies. These groups allow for easy viewing and management with additional built in functionality allowing Company Lists to be accessed for mass adding of Counterparties for Engagements (see Add Counterparties).

Access Company Lists

Create New Company List

  1. Click "Company Lists" link in the Quick Links sections
  2. <New Company List>
  3. Enter the name for the list
  4. <Save>
  5. <Add Company>
  6. Search for companies using the filter or get companies from an existing list. See Add Counterparties for more details about the search filter and Company List search
  7. When all the filters are in place, click <Search>
  8. When the company list renders, click the box next to the companies you wish to add
  9. Click <Add Selected Records To ...>
  10. Update filters and repeat steps to add additional companies
  11. <Back> to return to the Company List page

Update Existing Company Lists

Edit the Company List Name

  1. <Company Lists> under Quick Links
  2. Click the "Edit" link.
  3. Update the list name.
  4. <Save>

Delete a Company List

  1. <Company Lists> under Quick Links
  2. Click the "Del" link.
  3. <OK>
