Email Notification

This functionality allows HL staff members to send email notifications to other staff members alerting them that an activity in Salesforce took place.

  1. Lookup the contact
  2. Hover over the Activities "Quick-Link"
  3. Click "View" for the activity you wish to run the report
  4. Click <Send Notification>
  5. Click to the right of the "To" field and search for the recipient to send the notification
  6. Expand the "Additional CCs" area to add additional recipients
  7. Click <Choose File> to add an attachment
  8. Update the Subject and Email Body as needed
  9. <Send Email>

To save time in steps 5 & 6, distribution lists can be setup. Please contact SalesforceSupport@HL.com for details.
