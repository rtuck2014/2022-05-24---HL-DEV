CapIQ Company Information

Access the CapIQ Company Page

  1. Lookup the Sponsor company in Salesforce
  2. Scroll down to the "CapIQ Information" section
  3. Click the company link (CapIQ Portfolio Company column)

View Additional CapIQ Company Information

Scroll the page to view other sections including:

  • Related Companies
  • Investment Sponsors
  • Investment Companies
  • Company History
  • Financial Sponsor Opportunities & Engagements
  • Funds

Add CapIQ Company to Salesforce

You can add a CapIQ company to Salesforce directly from the CapIQ company record (see the "Add New CapIQ Company to Salesforce" section in Create a New Company).
