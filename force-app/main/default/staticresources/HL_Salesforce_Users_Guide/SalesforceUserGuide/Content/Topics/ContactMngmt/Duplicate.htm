Duplicate Contact and Company Records

Note: For duplicate opportunities and engagements, you will need to submit an IT Support Ticket.

How to Indicate a Duplicate Contact

Watch the How-To Video
  1. Correct contact record:
    1. Update the Flag Reason to "Duplicate Contact"
    2. Update the Flag Reason Comment to "Survivor"
  2. Old duplicate contact records:
    1. Update the Flag Reason to "Duplicate Contact"
    2. Update the Flag Reason Comment to "Merge into survivor"
  3. Repeat step 2 if there are more than one old duplicate record

How to Indicate a Duplicate Company

Watch the How-To Video

Follow the same procedure for contacts except using the Flag Reason and Flag Reason Comment field on the company page.
