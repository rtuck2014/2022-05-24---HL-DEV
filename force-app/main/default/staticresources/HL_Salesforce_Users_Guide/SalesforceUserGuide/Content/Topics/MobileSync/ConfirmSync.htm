Note:

iOS software (Apple) will often automatically link contacts with the same first name and last name

even if they are different.

To unlink contacts:

Open the contact that has been automatically linked.

  1. At the bottom of the contact screen, you will see all the contacts that are linked
  2. Tap on the red minus sign of the contact to unlink
