Manage Relationships (Mass Relationship Creator)

The Mass Relationship creator allows you to quickly add a relationship to an HL staff member for all non-HL team members on a deal including the external team and counterparties.

  1. Lookup the engagement.
  2. <Manage Relationships> (top of page)
  3. Type the HL staff member name that you wish to add the relationships in the field
  4. Use radio buttons to filter results (External Team, Client Team or Counterparty Contacts)
  5. Select a "Strength Rating" for each contact you wish to add a relationship
  6. Optional - check the "Sync to Outlook" box to sync to your Outlook (only works if you currently sync your Outlook)
  7. When finished, click <Back to Engagement>

 If the relationship did not save, try adding a "Strength Rating" or check the "Sync to Outlook" box.
