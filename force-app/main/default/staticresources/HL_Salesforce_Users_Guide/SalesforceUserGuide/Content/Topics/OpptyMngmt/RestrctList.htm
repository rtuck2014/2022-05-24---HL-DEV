Add Company(s) to the Restricted List

Note: For information whether a company should be restricted, please consult the Code of Ethics.

Adding a Company to the Restricted List

  1. Lookup the opportunity.
  2. <Restricted List> (top of the page).
  3. Once in the Compliance Portal, click <Add Company to Restricted List>
  4. Enter ALL required information and all known non-required information.
  5. <Save and Close> or to add another company, <Save and Add>.

Adding MNPI (Material Non-Public Information)

Important: The indication of Material Non-Public Information is mandatory. Please note, if you select "YES" to MNPI in Salesforce, it will automatically add the company to the Restricted List and adding it again following the steps above will result in a duplicate request.

  1. Select “YES” to indicate the Firm is in possession of MNPI. Select “NO” to indicate the Firm does not have MNPI.
  2. When “YES” is selected for MNPI, the “Authorized By” field will appear alongside the MNPI button. Enter the name of the authorizing person as usual.
  3. When “NO” is selected for MNPI, the “Authorized By” field is not required and will not appear.

Removing a Request

  1. Lookup the opportunity.
  2. <Restricted List> (top of the page).
  3. Click the "Remove Request" link.

Automatic Removal

Opportunities

For restricted "dead" opportunities, the restricted companies will be removed in 14 days. For opportunities not restricted, the restricted status will be immediately changed to "Not Restricted".

Engagements

For restricted engagements marked "accounting closed" it will stay on the restricted list until 90 days. For engagements not restricted, the restricted status will be immediately changed to "Not Restricted".
