Affiliated Companies

Did you know you can find out if a contact is affiliated with different companies in Saleforce? Want to know if they are an attorney or accountant, consultant or advisor, board member or even a former employee? Check out the Affiliated Company area.

Where To Look

  1. Lookup the contact
  2. Hover over the "Affiliated Companies" quick-link

Add Additional Information

We encourage all staff members to add affiliated company information whenever possible.

  1. Lookup the contact
  2. Hover over the "Affiliated Companies" quick-link
  3. Click <New Affiliation>
  4. Search for the affiliated company (has to be in Salesforce)
  5. Enter Status
  6. Enter Type (use picklist)
  7. Enter any additional information (like start/end date, notes, etc.)
  8. <Save>
