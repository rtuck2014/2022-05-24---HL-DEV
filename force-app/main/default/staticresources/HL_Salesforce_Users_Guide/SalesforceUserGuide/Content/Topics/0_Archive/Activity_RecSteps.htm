Recommended Steps

Please note, you must be first setup to sync to Outlook before you can sync activities. Please contact SalesforceSupport@HL.com to get setup.

The activities functionality in Salesforce allows staff members to record interactions with contacts. Outlook Integration syncs Salesforce activities and Outlook calendar events.

Future Activities

  1. STEP 1 - Create the meeting invite in Outlook
    1. Create the meeting in Outlook
    2. Tag meeting - Category = Salesforce
    3. Send the meeting invitation
    4. Salesforce will match up the meeting attendees with email addresses in Salesforce and create an activity for any matching contact records.
  2. STEP 2 - After the Meeting
    1. Lookup the contact
    2. Hover over the "Activities" Quick-Link and select "Edit" for the appropriate activity to update
    3. Enter the meeting notes in the Internal Notes field (these notes do not sync to Outlook and are only available in Salesforce)
    4. Check the "Schedule Follow-up" box to create a follow-up activity. (Check Sync to Outlook to create a calendar event for the Primary HL Attendee)
    5. <Save>
  3. STEP 3 - Send Notifications to other HL staff members. See Email Notification
  4. STEP 4 - See Activity Report for instructions on how to print out a hard copy of the meeting details

 

Past Activities

For activities that have already occurred, the following steps are recommended:

  1. STEP 1 - Create the activity in Salesforce
  2. STEP 2 - Send out activity notifications to other HL staff members
  3. STEP 3 - Create an Outlook calendar event for the activity or follow-up (see Sync to Outlook)
  4. STEP 4 - Send out the synced follow-up meeting invitation manually in Outlook.
