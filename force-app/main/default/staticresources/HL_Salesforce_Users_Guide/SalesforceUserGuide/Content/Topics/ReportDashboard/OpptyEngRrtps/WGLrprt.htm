Working Group List Report

Watch the How-To Video

This report will enable you to customize and run working group lists for Opportunities and Engagements.

How to Run the Working Group List Report

  1. Lookup the Opportunity or Engagement you wish to run the report.
  2. Click <Opportunity Reports> or <Engagement Reports> button.
  3. Select the "Opportunity Working Group List" or "Engagement Working Group List" report link.
  4. Reordering:
    1. Role - Click & drag the Role to the appropriate location
    2. Company - Expand "Role" then click & drag the company
    3. Contact - Expand the Company (within Role) then click & drag the contact.
  5. <View Report>
