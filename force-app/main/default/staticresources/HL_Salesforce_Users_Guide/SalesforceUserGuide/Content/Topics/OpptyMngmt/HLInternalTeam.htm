HL Internal Team

The HL Internal Team are HL staff members working on the opportunity or engagement.

Add New Team Members

  1. Lookup the opportunity

  2. Hover over the "HL Internal Team" Quick Link
  3. <Modify Roles>
  4. Type the staff name in the "Staff" field
  5. Select the appropriate name from the automatic drop list
  6. Check all roles that apply to that team member
  7. <Save>
  8. Repeat for all team members
  9. <Save>
  10. <Return to Opportunity>

Edit Existing Team Members

  1. Lookup the Opportunity

  2. Hover over the "HL Internal Team" Quick Link
  3. <Modify Roles>
  4. "Uncheck" or "check" existing team member roles accordingly
  5. <Save>
  6. <Return to Opportunity>
