Response Types

Salesforce uses a third-party application which automatically adds the defined response list to a campaign when it's created. Response types are pre-set based on Campaign Type. For special cases requiring custom responses, please contact Marketing - Direct Response.

Contact preferences and/or additional notes related to the conf/event can be captured in the campaign response "comments" area.
