Counterparty Tracking

Although counterparties can be tracked at both the opportunity and engagement level, some functionality will not be available at the opportunity level (e.g. bid tracking.)

Mass Update

  1. Lookup the opportunity/engagement
  2. <Counterparties> (top of the page)
  3. Check the box next to the counterparty(s) you wish to update
  4. Enter "Type", "Tier", "Dates" and any "Comments" (this will populate all counterparty rows you checked)
  5. <Save>

Edit Counterparty Information (Single Update)

  1. Lookup the opportunity/engagement
  2. <Counterparties> (top of the page)
  3. Click the "Edit" link for the appropriate counterparty
  4. Update the information
  5. <Save> or <Save and New> to add a counterparty

In addition to Progress Dates, this area allows you to change the actual counterparty company as well as other information like Coverage Officers, Primary Calling Responsibility, Rationale Notes, Reason for Decline and Counterparty Name.

Update Counterparty Contact

  1. Lookup the opportunity/engagement.
  2. <Counterparties> (top of the page)
  3. Click the "Details" link for the appropriate counterparty
  4. Hover over the "Opportunity Counterparty Contacts" or "Engagement Counterparty Contacts"Quick Link
  5. <New Opportunity Counterparty Contact> or <New Engagement Counterparty Contact>
  6. Click next to the contact field
  7. Lookup and select the appropriate contact (click the name link)
  8. Enter the "Type" from the drop list.
  9. <Save> or <Save and New> to add another contact
