Move Contact

Watch the How-To Video

Important: You must first check to see if you have move rights (see steps below). If not, contact SalesforceSupport@HL.com to request move rights.

Do I have Move Rights?

If you have move rights

Quick Tip: Save yourself some time! First check to see if the company (that the contact has moved to) is in Salesforce before starting the move process.

  1. Lookup the contact record to be moved
  2. "Edit" the Company Name field (double-click the field directly)
  3. Click and lookup the company they have moved to (if the company doesn't exist, you will need to create a new company record and start the process over)
  4. Update contact information appropriately (phone, email, and fax - the contact’s address will update to their new company’s address automatically when saved if "Inherit Company Address" box is checked
  5. <Save>

If you do not have move rights

Quick Tip: Save yourself some time! First check to see if the company (that the contact has moved to) is in Salesforce before starting the move process.

  1. Create a new contact record at the company they have moved to (if the company doesn't exist, you will need to create a new company record and start the process over)
    1. In the Flag Reason field, select "Duplicate Contact"
    2. In the Flag Reason Comment field, enter "Survivor"
  2. <Save>
  3. Edit the old contact record
    1. In the Flag Reason field, select "Contact has left company"
    2. In the Flag Reason Comment field enter "Now works for <enter new company name>"
  4. <Save>
