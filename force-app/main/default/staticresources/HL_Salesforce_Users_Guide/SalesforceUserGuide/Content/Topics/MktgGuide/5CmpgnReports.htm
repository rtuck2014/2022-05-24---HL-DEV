Campaign Reports

Direct Marketing (DM) is the owner of the marketing campaign templates reports. The templates can be re-purposed and shared (see Share a Report Folder ) or even saved as your own if you want to make adjustments. It is important that these templates are edited by DM only because the layouts need to stay consistent due to constant merging of spreadsheets during the list building process.

For detailed instruction on how to customize a report, please refer to the section Customize Reports.

Want to "pin" a frequently used report folder for easy access or share a report? Please see Reports.

Report Templates

There are several templates provided to assist with the management of marketing campaigns.

Template Name Best Suited for...
TEMPLATE - 2015 [xx] Conference_ATTENDANCE View all member who attendees (post conference/event)
TEMPLATE - 2015 [xx] Conference_ALL View all members of the conference/event campaign
TEMPLATE - 2015 [xx] Conference_RESP Viewing responses for conferences/events
TEMPLATE - 2015 [xx] Conference_NO RESP Viewing no responses for conferences/events
TEMPLATE - RB Mailing ALL Mailing list campaigns (non-conference/events)

To access templates:

  1. Click "Reports" tab
  2. Click "Marketing Reports" (left side under folders)
  3. Click the link of the template to view or click to quickly access "customize" or "export" options
