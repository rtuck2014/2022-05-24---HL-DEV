Mobile Versus Syncing

Mobile

  • Utilizes the Salesforce Mobile app
  • Search and view all Salesforce data like:
    • Contacts
    • Companies
    • Opportunities
    • Engagements
    • Activities
    • Dashboards
    • Reports
  • Can create new records

Syncing

  • Should be considered a complementary feature and not an alternative to Salesforce1.
  • You must opt-in to syncing and have IT set it up for you.
  • Contacts with your relationship are pushed down from Salesforce to Outlook and then to your mobile device if you setup to sync.
  • You will only be able to view and search contacts that you have a relationship with in Salesforce.
  • You can't create new contacts in Outlook, they must be created in Salesforce or Salesforce1.
  • You can update existing contact information in Outlook and it will sync back up to Salesforce.
