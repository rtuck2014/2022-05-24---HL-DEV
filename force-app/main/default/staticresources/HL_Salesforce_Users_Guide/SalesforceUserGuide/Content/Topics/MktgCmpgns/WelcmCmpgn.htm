What are Campaigns?

Campaigns are marketing initiatives like mailing lists, conferences, events, deal announcements, etc. managed in Salesforce.

Campaign Structure and Hierarchy

Campaigns are structured via Parent, Sub-Parent and Child campaigns.

As it appears in Salesforce:

Who manages Campaigns?

The Marketing - Direct Response Team (in coordination with the lines of businesses) creates and manages the campaigns in Salesforce.

How do Staff Members Assist with Campaigns?

Typically, staff members will be assisting with conferences, events and mailing lists. Below are the common tasks associated:

  • Add a contact to a campaign
  • Add a response for a contact to a campaign
  • Manage the conference or event name badge information
  • View & download the contact list for a campaign
