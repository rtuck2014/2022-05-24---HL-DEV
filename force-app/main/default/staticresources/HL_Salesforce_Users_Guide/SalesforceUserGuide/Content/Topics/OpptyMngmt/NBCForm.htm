Submit the NBC (New Business Committee) Form

Note: Be sure to check out the CF New Business Committee Policy located on the HL Intranet (CF/Policies).

  1. Lookup the opportunity.

  2. <NBC Form> (top of the page).
  3. Enter form information.
  4. <Save NBC>
  5. If then form is ready for submission, click <Submit for Review> and the form will be forwarded to the New Business Committee.
