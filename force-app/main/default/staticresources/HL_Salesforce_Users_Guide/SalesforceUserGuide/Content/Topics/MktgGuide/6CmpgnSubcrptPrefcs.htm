Subscription Preferences

The Subscription Preferences section allows us to indicate the contacts mailing preferences including the ability to opt contacts out of specific campaign types. When a subscription preference field is updated, a "change date" will be recorded.

Types of Subscription Preferences

Field If checked, the contact will be "opted-out" of...
Do Not Solicit All campaign types
Do Not Email All email campaigns (If selected, the contact should also be "opted-out" of one of the below campaign types)
Deal Announcements All deal announcement campaigns
Events/Conferences All event and conference campaigns
General Announcements All firm announcement campaigns
Insights/Content All insight and content campaigns

How to Update Subscription Preferences

  1. Lookup the contact in Salesforce
  2. Scroll down to the Subscription Preferences section
  3. Update the appropriate Subscription Preferences field accordingly
  4. <Save>
