Create an Activity in Salesforce

An Activity is a way to record interactions with a contact (like a meeting or call). Follow the steps below to enter in an activity.

  1. Lookup the contact.

  2. Mouseover the "Activities" quick-link then click <Add Activity>

  3. Enter the Required Activity Details:
    1. Type
    2. Subject
    3. Date
  4. Enter Meeting notes in the Internal Notes field
  5. Other options:
    1. Private- check this box to limit access (primary HL Attendee and their delegate)
    2. Sync to Outlook - if you wish to sync to outlook, please see Create Activity via Outlook.
    3. Notify Coverage Team - this is checked by default and will automatically send an email notification to the HL staff who cover the company, notifying them of the activity.
  6. Add External Attendees "non-HL" contacts to the activity
  7. Add HL Employees - staff members to the activity
    • For Administrative Assistants adding an activity for their officer:
      1. Add your officer as an HL Attendee
      2. Flag your officer as the "Primary" HL Attendee
      3. Remove yourself as an HL Attendee (click "del" link)
  8. Companies Discussed - Link Salesforce company records that were discussed during the activity (click <Add New Company> if the company record does not yet exist).
  9. Related Opportunities - Link Opportunity records that were discussed during the activity.
  10. Schedule Followup (we highly recommend creating an activity in Outlook if you want to sync to Outlook but you can create a follow-up directly in Salesforce following the steps below)
    1. Check "Schedule Follow-up" box
    2. Enter date/time for the follow-up activity
    3. Enter notes in the "Follow-up Comments" field
    4. Check "Sync To Outlook" to sync the follow-up activity to your Outlook calendar. Please note, this only creates an Outlook event for the "Primary" HL Attendee.
  11. <Save>

Edit an Activity in Salesforce

  1. Lookup the contact in Salesforce
  2. Click on the Activities "Quick-Link" at the top
  3. Click "Edit" for the activity you wish to edit
  4. Update fields accordingly
  5. <Save>
