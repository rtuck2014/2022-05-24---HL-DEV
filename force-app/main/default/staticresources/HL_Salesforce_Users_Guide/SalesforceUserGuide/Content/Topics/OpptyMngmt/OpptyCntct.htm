Opportunity Contacts

Add New Opportunity Contacts

  1. Lookup the opportunity
  2. Click the "Opportunity Contacts" Quick Link
  3. <Add Opportunity Contact>
  4. Click the for the contact field and select the new opportunity contact
  5. <Save>

Edit Existing Opportunity Contacts

  1. Lookup the opportunity
  2. Click the "Opportunity Contacts" Quick Link
  3. Click "Edit" next to the appropriate team member
  4. Make changes
  5. <Save>

Delete Existing Opportunity Contacts

  1. Lookup the opportunity
  2. Click the "Opportunity Contacts" Quick Link
  3. Click "Del" link next to the appropriate team member
  4. <OK>
