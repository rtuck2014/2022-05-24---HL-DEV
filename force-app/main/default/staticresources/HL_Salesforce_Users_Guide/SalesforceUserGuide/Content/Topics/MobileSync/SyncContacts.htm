Syncing Contacts

First time sync? Contact SalesforceSupport@HL.com to setup your device and profile.

How are synced contacts identified?

Synced contacts will have a category of "Salesforce" in Outlook.

Where are my synced contacts located?

In the default contact folder (most common)

Typically, contacts are synced to the default contact folder in Outlook. The synced contacts can be identified by searching for those with a Category = "Salesforce"

In the Salesforce folder

If you have a Salesforce contacts folder, they will be synced there.

How do I sync a contact?

Contacts with your relationship will automatically be synced (Manage Relationship):

  1. You must have a relationship with the contact in Salesforce
  2. The contact record must also be checked to "Sync to Outlook" (this is checked by default when a new relationship is added)

How do I un-sync a contact?

There are two ways to un-sync a contact:

  1. From Outlook - delete the contact in your Salesforce contacts folder (this only removes the sync and does not delete the contact in Salesforce)
  2. From Salesforce - un-check the "Sync to Outlook" box in the HL Relationship area (see below)

What Information Syncs?

  • Basic contact information (name, address, email, etc.)
  • Notes
  • Categories

Personal Notes

You can't create a new record or move a record in Outlook, that must be done directly in Salesforce.
